Bio

I have received my PhD in Computer Science and Systems Engineering (curriculum: computer science) from Department of Informatics, Bioengineering, Robotics and Systems Engineering (DIBRIS), University of Genova, Italy in 2022 under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Marco Maratea. I have done MS in Information Technology from Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, University of Applied Sciences, Lemgo, Germany in 2009. I have done my Bachelors in Computer Science (Hons) from Department of Computer Science, University of Peshawar in 2004.

Currently, I am working as an Assistant Professor in Department of Computer Science/IT, Sarhad University of Science and Information Technology (SUIT), Peshawar, Pakistan. I am teaching different courses at bachelor, master and PhD level.

I have an academic, research and industrial experience of more than 10 years.

My research interests include: Artificial Intelligence, Knowledge Representation and Reasoning, Automated Planning and Scheduling, Combinatorial Optimization, Logic Programming, Answer Set Programming, Low Power Wide Area Network technologies (NB-IoT, Sigfox, LoRa), Futuristic intelligence using Internet of Things (IoT) based Wireless Personal Area Networks, Computer Aided Simulations for Wireless Networks
